🧠 The Story Behind This Project

In today’s business environment, organizations generate large amounts of sales data every day. However, raw data alone does not provide meaningful insights. The goal of this project was to transform raw sales data into an interactive and visually understandable dashboard using Power BI.
This project was created to practice data analysis and visualization skills by exploring sales trends, understanding business performance, and identifying patterns that can support better decision-making.
________________________________________

🔍 What This Dashboard Explores

This dashboard focuses on analyzing sales performance across different time periods, product categories, and business types.

It answers important business questions such as:
•	How do sales change over time (year, quarter, and month)?
•	Which product categories generate the highest sales?
•	How do different business types contribute to total sales?
•	What trends can be identified from sales amounts and due dates?
By visualizing these aspects, the dashboard helps users quickly understand overall sales performance.

________________________________________

📈 Key Insights from the Data

Some important insights that can be identified from the dashboard include:
•	Sales Trend Over Time: Sales amounts vary across months, showing periods of higher and lower business performance.
•	Category Contribution: Certain product categories contribute significantly more to total sales than others.
•	Business Type Performance: Different business types such as specialty bike shops, resellers, and warehouses contribute differently to revenue.
•	Due Date Analysis: Comparing sales amount with due date helps track how revenue is distributed over time.
These insights help businesses identify opportunities for growth and improve strategic planning.
